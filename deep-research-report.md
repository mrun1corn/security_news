# Executive Summary  
This report surveys authoritative sources in virtualization, infrastructure, DevOps, cybersecurity, and antivirus, identifying the top 12 sites/feeds in each category. Sources include vendor blogs, news sites, aggregators, and threat feeds. For each we detail its focus, content type (e.g. news, tutorials, threat intel), update frequency, audience, credibility (e.g. traffic rank, citations), and RSS/feeds. We rank sources by popularity (web traffic, social followers) and credibility (vendor reputation, citations, longevity) within each category, explaining our criteria. A consolidated table highlights overlap across categories. We conclude with a recommended 10-item daily/weekly reading list and suggest an automated RSS aggregation setup (with tags/filters) to keep abreast of updates. Throughout, we cite official/primary sources and original publications.
